ℹ️ About SeaSense

SeaSense gives Israeli coastal water sports enthusiasts a clear, colour-coded marine forecast — so you can decide in seconds whether it's safe to go out.

🌊 What it shows
  • Wind speed & gusts — direction, bearing, and arrow for every 3-hour slot
  • Wave height & direction — from the ECMWF WAM ocean model
  • Air temperature — so you know what to wear
  • Rain probability & amount — per time slot and daily summary
  • Sunrise, sunset & UV index — via the ☀️ Sun & UV panel
  • GO / CAUTION / DANGER status — instantly at a glance, customisable thresholds
📡 Wind models
ICON EU Recommended
German model (~7 km resolution) covering Europe & the Mediterranean. Best for coastal breezes, sea-land thermal shifts, and same-day decisions at Israeli beaches.
ECMWF IFS
European global model (~25 km). Gold standard for multi-day accuracy. Excellent for planning trips 2–5 days ahead when storm systems or weather fronts approach.
GFS
US global model (~25 km). Best for extended outlooks beyond 5 days and cross-referencing with other models. Less detail near coastlines than ICON EU.
🌊 Wave data always comes from ECMWF WAM — the dedicated ocean wave model, regardless of which wind model is selected.
⚙️ Customisation
  • Set your own CAUTION and DANGER wind & wave thresholds from the sidebar
  • Switch between knots and km/h
  • Choose from 11 Israeli coastal locations or enter custom coordinates
  • Share your exact view with friends via the 🔗 Share button
  • Data auto-refreshes every 15 minutes
All data is free — no API key required.
